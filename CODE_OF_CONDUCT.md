# Community Code of Conduct

PhishGuard AI is committed to a respectful, inclusive, and technically
constructive community.

## Expected Behavior

- Discuss ideas and code with patience and professional respect.
- Give specific, actionable feedback.
- Welcome questions and first-time contributors.
- Credit research, code, testing, and review appropriately.
- Handle security data carefully and use synthetic examples when possible.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, threats, or personal attacks
- Publishing another person's private information
- Deliberately introducing unsafe code, credentials, or active malicious data
- Plagiarism or misrepresenting another person's work
- Repeated disruption of technical discussions or reviews

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns privately to
[omobolaji.adeyan@gmail.com](mailto:omobolaji.adeyan@gmail.com). Reports will
be reviewed by lead maintainer Omobolaji Adeyan. The project may remove
content, close discussions, reject contributions, or restrict participation
when necessary to protect the community.

Security vulnerabilities should follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
